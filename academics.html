Academics

Building Strong Foundations for a Bright Future

Academic Excellence

At Mamta Saraswati Balika Vidya Mandir, we provide a well-structured academic curriculum that focuses on knowledge, creativity, critical thinking, and character development. Our experienced faculty members ensure that every student receives quality education and personalized attention.

Classes Offered

Primary Section

Nursery to Class V with activity-based learning, language development, and foundational education.

Middle Section

Classes VI to VIII focusing on conceptual learning, practical activities, and skill development.

Secondary Section

Classes IX to X with emphasis on board preparation, analytical thinking, and academic excellence.

Senior Secondary

Classes XI and XII offering Science, Commerce, and Humanities streams for career development.
