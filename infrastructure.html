Our Infrastructure

Providing Modern Facilities for Quality Education

World-Class Learning Environment

Mamta Saraswati Balika Vidya Mandir offers a safe, modern, and student-friendly environment designed to support academic excellence, creativity, and overall development. Our campus is equipped with facilities that enhance learning and encourage students to achieve their full potential.

Campus Facilities

🏫 Smart Classrooms

Spacious and well-ventilated classrooms equipped with modern teaching aids and digital learning tools.

📚 Library

A rich collection of academic books, reference materials, magazines, and newspapers for students.

💻 Computer Lab

Modern computer laboratory with internet access and practical training facilities.

🔬 Science Laboratory

Well-equipped Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs for practical experiments and research activities.

⚽ Sports Ground

Large playground for outdoor games, sports training, and physical fitness activities.

🚌 Transport Facility

Safe and reliable transportation services covering nearby areas for students and staff.

Safety & Security

🎥 CCTV Surveillance

Complete campus monitoring through CCTV cameras for student safety and security.

👩 Female Staff Support

Dedicated female staff members available to guide and support students whenever required.

🚑 First Aid Facility

Basic medical assistance and first-aid facilities available on campus.

Additional Facilities

🎨 Activity Room

Dedicated space for art, craft, cultural programs, and creative activities.

🎤 Seminar Hall

Spacious hall for workshops, seminars, competitions, and school functions.

🌳 Green Campus

Eco-friendly environment with gardens and open spaces that promote healthy learning.
